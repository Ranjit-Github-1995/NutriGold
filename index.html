WHO Marketing code

The promotion of baby nutrition is a sensitive subject due to the vulnerability of newbornbabies. They need to be nurtured and protected by their parents and other stakeholders. Toensure that every stake- holder acts in the newborn’s best interest, the World Health Organization formulated the International Code of Marketing of Breast Milk substitutes. This WHOCode of ethics contains guidelines and regulationsconcerning marketing activities for BreastMilk substitutes. Many local and regional governments used this code as a basis to develop heir own legislation.Being an involved and genuine company, Biniton International agrees with the motives behind the WHO Code of Ethics
